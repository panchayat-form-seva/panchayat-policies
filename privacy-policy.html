Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Panchayat Form Seva respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Username
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Address
  • Village
  • Taluka
  • District

This information is securely stored on our servers for account management and application functionality.

Form Information

When you fill out Panchayat forms, the information is used only to generate PDF documents.

We do not permanently store this form information on our servers.

PDF Generation

The data submitted in forms is temporarily processed on our server only for PDF generation.

After the PDF is generated, it is saved to the storage location selected by the user on their own device.

We do not permanently store the generated PDF files on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your account information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate your login.
  • Improve application functionality.
  • Provide customer support.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

Passwords are stored in encrypted (hashed) form and are never stored as plain text.

Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate information.
  • Keeping your login credentials secure.
  • Protecting the PDF files stored on your device.

Third-Party Services

The application may use trusted third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers process data only as necessary to operate the application.

Children's Privacy

Panchayat Form Seva is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will become effective once published in the application.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the contact information provided within the application.
